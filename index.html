Our Story

PhoolandCo began as a small passion project in 2018, when our founder Priya started creating floral arrangements for friends and family. What started as a hobby quickly blossomed into a full-fledged business as demand grew for our unique, handmade creations.

Today, we're a team of talented artisans dedicated to crafting beautiful floral products that bring joy to people's lives. Each piece is made with love, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality that you can feel in every petal.
